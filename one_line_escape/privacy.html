Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06 February 2026

Thank you for playing One Line Escape. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile game.

Information We Collect

One Line Escape does not require users to create an account or provide any personal information.

However, certain information may be collected automatically through third-party services used in the app.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • App usage data (levels played, interactions, crash reports)
  • Advertising identifiers (used for displaying ads)

This information is used only to improve game performance, stability, and user experience.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Play Services

These services operate under their own privacy policies. You can review Google’s Privacy Policy here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Advertisements

One Line Escape displays advertisements to support ongoing development. Ad providers may use cookies or device identifiers to show relevant ads.

Users can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: https://adssettings.google.com

Data Security

We do not store or manage personal user data on our own servers. Any data collected by third-party services is protected using industry-standard security measures.

Children’s Privacy

One Line Escape does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
