IPL 2026 Playoffs Schedule Announced

By VB Bingo Blog • May 02, 2026 • 3 min read

The TATA IPL 2026 playoffs schedule has officially been announced, and fans are eagerly waiting for the final stage of the tournament. The playoffs will feature exciting matches across multiple venues, leading up to the grand finale in Ahmedabad.

IPL 2026 Playoffs Schedule

Match Date Venue
Qualifier 1 May 26 Dharamshala
Eliminator May 27 New Chandigarh
Qualifier 2 May 29 New Chandigarh
Final May 31 Ahmedabad (Narendra Modi Stadium)

Key Highlights

  • The IPL 2026 final will be held in Ahmedabad.
  • Qualifier matches are split between Dharamshala and New Chandigarh.
  • Narendra Modi Stadium will host the grand finale.
  • High-voltage knockout matches expected.

Why Ahmedabad is Special?

Ahmedabad’s Narendra Modi Stadium is the largest cricket stadium in the world. It has hosted several major IPL and international matches, making it a perfect venue for the grand finale of IPL 2026.

Conclusion

The IPL 2026 playoffs promise thrilling cricket action as teams battle for the trophy. With the final set in Ahmedabad, fans can expect a massive showdown at one of the most iconic stadiums in the world.
