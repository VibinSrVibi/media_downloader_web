India A vs Afghanistan A Match Preview 2026: Prediction, Pitch Report, Playing XI & Key Players

By VB Bingo Blog • Jun 17, 2026 • 3 min read

India A will face Afghanistan A in the fifth match of the Tri-Nation A Series 2026 at the Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium, Sri Lanka. Both teams are looking to strengthen their position in the tournament, making this an important contest.

Match Details

  • Match: India A vs Afghanistan A
  • Tournament: Tri-Nation A Series 2026
  • Match Number: 5th Match
  • Venue: Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium, Dambulla
  • Date: June 17, 2026
  • Format: ODI (50 Overs)

India A Team Preview

India A enters this match with a talented squad featuring several exciting young cricketers. The team will rely on captain Tilak Varma and experienced batter Ruturaj Gaikwad to provide stability in the batting lineup.

Young opener Vaibhav Sooryavanshi has impressed fans with his aggressive approach and could play a key role at the top of the order.

Key Players for India A

  • Tilak Varma
  • Ruturaj Gaikwad
  • Vaibhav Sooryavanshi
  • Prabhsimran Singh
  • Anshul Kamboj

Afghanistan A Team Preview

Afghanistan A has shown impressive form throughout the tournament. Their batting lineup has displayed confidence, while their spin attack remains one of their strongest weapons in subcontinent conditions.

The Afghan side will be eager to continue their momentum and challenge India A once again.

Key Players for Afghanistan A

  • Bahir Shah
  • Hassan Eisakhil
  • Imran Mir
  • Abdullah Ahmadzai
  • Zahir Khan

Pitch Report

The Dambulla pitch generally offers a good balance between bat and ball. Batters can score freely during the first half of the innings, while spinners are expected to get assistance as the surface wears.

A first-innings score of around 270-300 runs could be competitive on this wicket.

Match Prediction

India A appears slightly stronger on paper due to their batting depth and overall experience. However, Afghanistan A has already shown they can challenge quality opposition and cannot be underestimated.

If India's top order gets off to a solid start, they should have a good chance of securing victory.

Predicted Winner

India A

Conclusion

The India A vs Afghanistan A clash promises an exciting battle between two talented sides. Fans can expect quality cricket, emerging stars, and a closely fought contest in Dambulla.
