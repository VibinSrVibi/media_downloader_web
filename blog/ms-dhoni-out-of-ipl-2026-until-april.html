MS Dhoni Suffers Calf Injury, Set to Miss IPL 2026 Matches Until April

By VB Bingo Blog • April 19, 2026 • 3 min read

MS Dhoni is likely to miss the early matches of IPL 2026 due to a calf injury, creating a major impact on Chennai Super Kings.

In a major setback for fans, MS Dhoni is likely to miss the early matches of IPL 2026 due to a calf injury. The legendary player remains a crucial part of Chennai Super Kings, and his absence could impact the team's performance.

What Happened?

Reports suggest that Dhoni suffered a calf injury during training sessions ahead of the tournament. While the injury is not considered serious, the team management is taking a cautious approach to ensure full recovery.

Recovery Timeline

Calf injuries usually require proper rest and rehabilitation. Dhoni is expected to be out of action until at least April 2026, depending on his recovery progress.

Impact on Chennai Super Kings

Dhoni’s absence creates a significant gap in leadership and finishing ability. Chennai Super Kings may face challenges in close matches without his experience.

Fan Reactions

Fans have expressed concern on social media but remain hopeful that Dhoni will return stronger later in the tournament.

Conclusion

While this injury sidelines Dhoni temporarily, it also provides an opportunity for other players to step up. All eyes will be on his recovery and return in IPL 2026.
