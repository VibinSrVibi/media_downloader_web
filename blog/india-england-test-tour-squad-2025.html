India squad for England Test series 2025

Last updated: May 2025

India squad for England Test series 2025 announced: Shubman Gill captains side, Rishabh Pant as Vice Captains, Karun Nair returns, Shami not included in the squad.

The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) on Saturday announced the squad for the five-match Test series against England from June 20

Shubman Gill was named the Test captain after Rohit Sharma announced his retirement from the format. Rishabh Pant as Vice Captians.

Karun Nair also earned a comeback into the Test side after seven years.

Mohammed Shami was not included in the 18-member team. Arshdeep Singh got his maiden Test call-up while Shardul Thakur returned to the fold. Kuldeep Yadav was the only specialist spinner who was a part of the side.

Squad

Shubman Gill (c), Rishabh Pant (wk/vc), Yashasvi Jaiswal, KL Rahul, Sai Sudharsan, Abhimanyu Easwaran, Karun Nair, Nitish Kumar Reddy, Ravindra Jadeja, Dhruv Jurel (wk), Washington Sundar, Shardul Thakur, Jasprit Bumrah, Mohammed Siraj, Prasidh Krishna, Akash Deep, Arshdeep Singh, Kuldeep Yadav.
